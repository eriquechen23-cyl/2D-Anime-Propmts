# Character Profiles: MUSCLE LINE-05 Squad

## Common Specs (共通規格)
*   **World**: 鋼脈都市 MUSCLE LINE (Muscle Line City)
*   **Identity**: 紋擊者 (Line Striker) / 鋼脈管理局 "MUSCLE LINE-05 Squad"
*   **Visual Theme**: Black Techwear Base + Character Theme Color Neon Lines.
*   **Power Source**: Muscle fiber resonance with Steel Pulse (紋壓) -> Visualized glowing patterns.

---

## 1. Kanzawa Retsu (鋼川 烈)
*   **Theme**: **Black + Red** (Explosion/Burst)
*   **Role**: Frontline Blaster / AoE Specialist
*   **Visuals**:
    *   **Age**: ~24.
    *   **Body**: Solid, thick upper body, defined pecs/delts. "Muscle Protagonist" vibe.
    *   **Outfit**: Black tight combat top, glowing red lines along collarbone/arms/side waist. Combat cargo pants + boots.
    *   **Weapon**: Red Cards & Dark Red Throwing Knives (engraved with explosive circuits).
*   **Abilities**:
    *   **Attribute**: Explosion Line (Red).
    *   **Style**: Tag enemies with cards/knives -> Accumulate charges -> Detonate all at once.
    *   **Signature**: Chain Explosion, Shockwave Control.
*   **Personality**: Straightforward, trash-talker during combat. Rivals with Ren. Scared of Renka's treatment.

## 2. Asakura Ren (朝倉 蓮)
*   **Theme**: **Black + Yellow** (Thunder/Charge)
*   **Role**: Heavy Melee Core / Anti-Large Specialist
*   **Visuals**:
    *   **Age**: ~23 (Mature).
    *   **Body**: Tall & bulky. Massive shoulders/traps/arms. "Power Forward" build.
    *   **Outfit**: Black sleeveless/short-sleeve tight suit, thin yellow circuit lines from collarbone to ribs. Optional tactical vest.
    *   **Weapon**: Black/Yellow Charged Great Axe (Thunder patterns on blade/handle).
*   **Abilities**:
    *   **Attribute**: Thunder Line (Yellow).
    *   **Style**: Frontline tanking -> Charge up -> Massive ground-splitting axe strike.
    *   **Signature**: Thunder Cleave, Super Armor Charge.
*   **Personality**: Serious, protective tank. Rivals with Retsu. Frequent customer of Renka's clinic due to tanking hits.

## 3. Kamiya Hayato (神谷 隼人)
*   **Theme**: **Black + Deep Blue** (Ice/Control)
*   **Role**: Mid-Long Range Control / Precision Marksman
*   **Visuals**:
    *   **Age**: ~21.
    *   **Body**: Lean athletic build, low body fat, defined but not bulky.
    *   **Outfit**: Black functional tight top + Deep Blue lines. Light tactical jacket, tech pants, tactical shoes.
    *   **Weapon**: Ice-Core Charge Gun (or Heavy Crossbow variant). Fires hexagonal ice-core rounds.
*   **Abilities**:
    *   **Attribute**: Ice Line (Blue).
    *   **Style**: "Tag" enemies to stack freeze layers -> Trigger Ice Burst at max stacks -> Chain reaction.
    *   **Signature**: Freeze Trap, Chain Detonation, Battlefield Control.
*   **Personality**: Calm, tactical brain. Analyzes terrain. Good synergy with Jin (Jin herds, Hayato nukes). Respects Renka.

## 4. Kazami Jin (風見 迅)
*   **Theme**: **Black + Green** (Wind/Speed)
*   **Role**: High Mobility Striker / Scout / Finisher
*   **Visuals**:
    *   **Age**: ~22.
    *   **Body**: Slender but tight muscles. Beautiful leg definition for speed/kicks.
    *   **Outfit**: High-spec black combat suit, thin neon green speed lines on sides/limbs. Parkour/Speed combat shoes.
    *   **Weapon**: Enhanced Gauntlets, Leg Boosters, Wind Pressure Emitters.
*   **Abilities**:
    *   **Attribute**: Wind Line (Green).
    *   **Style**: Flash steps (Shunpo), wind pressure disruption, hit-and-run.
    *   **Signature**: Afterimage Strike, Sonic Boom Kick.
*   **Personality**: Flirty/Playful, sharp instincts. Uses jokes to hide care. Pairs with Retsu for "Lure & Boom". Discusses tactics with Hayato.

## 5. Kurosaki Renka (黒崎 戀花)
*   **Theme**: **Black + Pink** (Medical/Buff/Violence)
*   **Role**: Combat Medic / Buffer / Intelligence
*   **Visuals**:
    *   **Age**: ~24 (Big Sister vibe).
    *   **Body**: Healthy, curvy, fit. Toned arms/thighs. Not fragile.
    *   **Outfit**: Black/Pink Street Medic style. Black jacket/crop top + Pink accents/medical symbols. Garters/Combat skirt/shorts.
    *   **Weapon**: Giant Syringe (Lance-like), Modified Med-kit, Line Infusion Gear.
*   **Abilities**:
    *   **Attribute**: Medical Line (Pink).
    *   **Style**: Buffs allies (Muscle Fiber Reinforcement) / Debuffs enemies (Neuro-paralysis). Smashes enemies with Giant Syringe if they get close.
    *   **Signature**: Adrenaline Overdrive, Poison/Heal Injection.
*   **Personality**: Sadistic doctor, sharp tongue but soft heart. Runs underground clinic. Scolds Retsu/Ren for getting hurt, then heals them aggressively.
